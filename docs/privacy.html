Privacy Policy

WorkItOut  ·  Last updated: April 30, 2025

WorkItOut is an indoor-cycling training app. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, where it goes, and how you can request deletion.

2. Third parties we share data with

Google Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase for authentication, cloud storage, and crash reporting:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages your sign-in (Google or anonymous). No passwords are stored by us.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your profile settings and ride history in Google's cloud so your data persists across devices.
  • Firebase Storage — stores your profile photo.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — receives anonymised crash reports (device model, OS version, stack trace). No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Anthropic (Anthropic, PBC)

When you request an AI-generated workout, we send your training parameters (FTP, weight, max HR, requested duration and target) to Anthropic's API to generate the interval plan. We do not send your name, email, or ride history to Anthropic. Anthropic processes this data under their Privacy Policy.

Strava (Strava Inc.) — optional

If you connect your Strava account, we:

  • Store an OAuth access token so we can upload activities on your behalf.
  • Upload completed ride data (time, distance, power, HR, GPS-equivalent virtual ride) to your Strava profile.

You can disconnect Strava at any time in Settings → Integrations. Disconnecting removes the token from our servers. Strava processes uploaded data under their Privacy Policy.

3. Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all Firestore records, Storage files, and the Strava token are permanently deleted within 30 days.

4. Data security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Firestore and Storage are protected by Firebase security rules that allow only authenticated users to access their own data.

5. Children's privacy

WorkItOut is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • Access or export your data by contacting us (see below).
  • Delete your account and data by signing in to the app and using Sign Out, then emailing us with a deletion request. We will process it within 30 days.
  • Disconnect Strava at any time in Settings → Integrations.
